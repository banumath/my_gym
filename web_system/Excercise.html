Over Head Press

Stand tall with a barbell across the front of your shoulders. Brace your core, then press the bar directly overhead. Lower it slowly back to the start.more

Push press

Using the same weight as in move 1A, bend your knees to create power to press the bar overhead. Then lower it slowly under complete control.more

Barbell shrug

keeping your arms straight, shrug the bar up so that your shoulders reach your ears. Hold this top position for a second, then lower it back to the start.more

Seated Arnold press

Dumbbell in each hand with palms facing you. Press them up overhead, rotating your wrists as you go, so you end with straight arms and palms facing away.more

Seated lateral raise

Switch to lighter dumbbells then, leaning forward slightly, raise them to shoulder height, leading with your elbows. Pause at the top, then lower back under control.more
